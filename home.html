How It Works

1

Brand Chooses Bottle Campaign

Brands select campaign objectives, target audience, and distribution strategy through our intuitive platform.

2

Label Designed + QR Linked

Custom labels with smart QR codes are designed and linked to branded content, AR experiences, and analytics dashboards.

3

Bottles Distributed

Smart bottles are distributed through both free sampling campaigns and retail partnerships for maximum reach.

4

Users Scan + Engage

Consumers scan QR codes to unlock AR content, participate in games, claim rewards, and share experiences.

5

Real-time Analytics Delivered

Brands receive comprehensive analytics on engagement, demographics, location data, and campaign performance.
